EU legislation in force in the fields of ATM/ANS, aerodromes and drones (SESLEX)

The following link provides with an overview of SESLEX:
https://learningzone.eurocontrol.int/doc/seslex.htm

It includes the following parts:

  • Part 1a: Basic Regulation 2018/1139, its implementing/delegated acts and associated DS/CS/AMC/GM – Focus on ATM/ANS (except conformity assessment of ATM/ANS equipment & ATCOs)
  • Part 1b: Basic Regulation 2018/1139, its implementing/delegated acts and associated DS/CS/AMC/GM – Focus on ATCOs & ATCO Training Organisations
  • Part 1c: Basic Regulation 2018/1139, its implementing/delegated acts and associated DS/CS/AMC/GM – Focus on Unmanned Aircraft Systems (UAS) & other fields (without DS/CS/AMC/GM), except ATM/ANS & Aerodromes
  • Part 1d: Basic Regulation 2018/1139, its implementing/delegated acts and associated DS/CS/AMC/GM – Focus on Aerodromes
  • Part 1e: Basic Regulation 2018/1139, its implementing/delegated acts and associated DS/CS/AMC/GM – Focus on conformity assessment of ATM/ANS equipment & Artificial Intelligence
  • Part 1f: Basic Regulation 2018/1139, its implementing/delegated acts and associated DS/CS/AMC/GM – Focus on cross-domains & fields other than ATM/ANS, Aerodromes & Drones (without DS/CS/AMC/GM)
  • Part 2a: Regulation 2024/2803 and its IAs & community specifications (except performance & charging schemes)
  • Part 2b: Regulation 2024/2803 and its IAs/AMCs/GM (focus on economic legislation: performance & charging schemes, procurement & rules on competition)
  • Part 3a: Other Relevant EU Legislation (security-related – Focus on civil aviation security)
  • Part 3b: Other Relevant EU Legislation (security-related – Other than civil aviation security)
  • Part 4: Other Relevant EU Legislation (airport-specific)
  • Part 5: Other Relevant EU Legislation (miscellaneous)
  • Part 6: Other Relevant EU Legislation (environment-specific)

EU regulatory developments in the fields of ATM/ANS, aerodromes and drones (FUTURELEX)

The following link provides with an overview of FUTURELEX:
https://learningzone.eurocontrol.int/doc/futurelex.htm It includes 2 parts:

  • Part 1
    • Part 1a (or page 1) lists the relevant EASA ToRs/NPAs/CRDs/Opinions (focus on ATM/ANS & cross-domain developments)
    • Part 1b (or page 2) lists the relevant EASA ToRs/NPAs/CRDs/Opinions (focus on Aerodromes, Drones & cross-domain developments)
  • Part 2 (or page 3) focuses on Ongoing Consultations & Future of the Single European Sky

Dates of applicability of pieces of legislation in the fields of ATM/ANS, aerodromes and drones not yet (fully) applicable (SESCAL)

The following link provides with an overview of SECAL:
https://learningzone.eurocontrol.int/doc/sescal.htm

It includes all pieces of legislation (regulations, decisions, directives, DS/CS/AMC/GM, etc.) listed in the overview of EU legislation in force in the fields of ATM/ANS, Aerodromes and Drones not yet (fully) applicable, marked with a timer in the latter overview. Once they become applicable, they will be removed from this overview.
