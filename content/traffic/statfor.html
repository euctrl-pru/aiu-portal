--- title: Traffic Forecasts description: "EUROCONTROL STATFOR works with stakeholders to produce forecasts of future network traffic." date: '2020-05-15' ---

The figure below presents the EUROCONTROL 7-year forecast (2025-2031) for air traffic movements (IFR) in millions of flights (Spring Edition 2025). This Seven-Year Forecast builds upon the Autumn 2024 publication and incorporates the latest flight trends, including a comprehensive update of the forecast inputs. The information is available by traffic zone (TZ) and includes actual traffic up to 2024 and different forecast scenarios (high, base, low) up to 2031. Simply select the TZ you are interested in from the drop-down menu to see the detailed information for each year.


For more information about the forecasts and the associated data, see the EUROCONTROL Forecast Update 2025-2031 publication.

Note: The traffic zone ‘Spain’ is the aggregated zone composed of ‘Spain-Canaries’ and ‘Spain-Continental’.
