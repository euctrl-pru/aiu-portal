--- title: EUROCONTROL Traffic Forecasts date: '2020-05-15' ---

EUROCONTROL works with stakeholders to produce forecasts of future network traffic.

The figure below presents the EUROCONTROL 7-year forecast (2023-2029) for air traffic movements (IFR) in millions of flights (Edition March 2023). The information is available by traffic zone (TZ) and includes actual traffic up to 2022 and different forecast scenarios (high, base, low) up to 2029. Simply select the TZ you are interested in from the drop-down menu to see the detailed information for each year.


For more information about the forecasts and the associated data, see the EUROCONTROL Forecast Update 2023-2029 publication.

Note: The traffic zone ‘Spain’ is the aggregated zone composed of ‘Spain-Canaries’ and ‘Spain-Continental’.
