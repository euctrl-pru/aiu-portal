--- title: EUROCONTROL Area slug: eurocontrol-area ---

The EUROCONTROL area consists of the 42 EUROCONTROL Member States.

In 2016, EUROCONTROL signed a Comprehensive Agreement with Israel and Morocco with a view to fully integrate both States into its working structures. Where available, data for those two States have been included in the analyses on this portal.
