PRC-ACI EUROPE APOC Exchange 2025

The PRC-ACI EUROPE APOC Exchange, hosted by the Performance Review Commission (PRC) of EUROCONTROL in collaboration with ACI EUROPE, was held on 10-11 March 2025 at EUROCONTROL headquarters in Brussels.

This engaging two-day event brought together 62 participants from 34 airports, providing a platform to exchange experiences, best practices, and lessons learned through interactive round tables covering a variety of APOC-related topics.

In addition to dynamic discussions, attendees benefited from insightful presentations by ACI and EUROCONTROL. These covered several key subjects, including the Airport Operations Centre Performance Study, conducted by the PRC as part of the Transformation Support Strategy.

All presentations from the event are now available for download on this page.
