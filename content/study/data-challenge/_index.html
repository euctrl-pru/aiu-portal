--- title: "PRC Data Challenges" type: bibliography date: "2025-09-01" ---

The Performance Review Commission (PRC) Data Challenge aims at engaging with data scientists to create teams and compete to solve interesting data problem for the Aviation community. The focus is on open data and open solutions in order to achieve wide distribution and reproducibility.
